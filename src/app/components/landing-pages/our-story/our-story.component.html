Who we are

What's our purpose?

Our founder, Amit, started this company with a clear vision: to bring every business online at an affordable cost. But it was his own frustrations—navigating the complex and often overwhelming process of turning digital ideas into reality—that sparked a deeper insight.

If seasoned tech professionals like him struggled to build software efficiently, how could entrepreneurs with brilliant ideas and limited technical know-how stand a chance? This realization became the foundation of our mission: to simplify the software development journey and make building digital solutions as easy and accessible as ordering a pizza.

From Frustration to Innovation

When Amit set out to build a simple business website, he didn’t expect the biggest hurdle would be the developers themselves. Missed deadlines, unclear communication, and unreliable execution turned his idea into a frustrating experience..

But instead of giving up, Amit had a brainwave: What if building software didn’t have to be this hard?

That moment of failure became the foundation for a bigger mission — to simplify and democratize software development for everyone, not just tech experts. What began as one idea has since evolved into a platform that helps anyone bring their digital vision to life — without the usual complexity.
