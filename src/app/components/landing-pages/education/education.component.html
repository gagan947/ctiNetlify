Master PTE

Master PTE is an AI-powered platform for students in Australia, also serving learners in India, to prepare for the PTE Academic Exam. It offers 98% accurate AI mock tests, plus audio and text practice with instant feedback to improve reading and speaking skills. Built with Angular JS, Node JS, and MySQL on AWS, it ensures a smooth, modern learning experience.

Audio & Text Practice with Instant Error Highlighting

Built with Angular JS (frontend), Node JS (backend), MySQL (database), and hosted on AWS, the platform delivers reliable performance and a seamless learning experience.
