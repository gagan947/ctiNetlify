Pre-launch: How it works in 6 steps

  • Step 1: After your initial consultation and payment confirmation, a dedicated team will be assembled to manage your app development.

  • Step 2: Your main point of contact will be a dedicated Product Specialist who will meet with you weekly and be available for ongoing collaboration through our platform. Meeting summaries and next actions are always documented.

  • Step 3: Throughout the development, your project manager will provide regular demos (every 2-3 weeks) of the latest app version, allowing you to give timely feedback on features and identify any issues.

  • Step 4: To ensure seamless operation, you'll grant your Product Specialist necessary access to your existing third-party services for integration.

  • Step 5: Upon completion, your Product Specialist will guide you through the process of publishing your app on major app stores like Google Play and the Apple App Store.

  • Step 6: For a full year from your project start date, you'll have access to our support team for any maintenance needs, ensuring your app remains updated, secure, and performs optimally.
