Privacy Policy & GDPR

ZYNQ cares about your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR, EU 2016/679).

Personal data we process:

  • Name, email, and other contact information you provide
  • Photos you upload in the app
  • Skin analysis result
  • Technical data such as device ID, IP address, and usage behavior (to improve service)

Purpose of processing:

  • Perform skin analysis and provide personalized recommendations
  • Create and manage your user account
  • Provide technical support and improve services
  • Fulfill legal obligations (e.g., accounting or regulatory inquiries)

Legal basis:

  • Your consent
  • Contract (terms of service)
  • Legal obligation
  • Legitimate interest (e.g., improving functionality)

Data retention:

We only store your data as long as necessary for the stated purpose or as required by law. You may request deletion at any time.

Third parties:

  • We never share your data with third parties without your explicit consent, except when required by law (e.g., tax authority).

Your rights:

  • Access your data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Object to processing
  • Request data portability
  • Restrict processing
  • File complaints with the Swedish Authority for Privacy Protection (IMY)

Data Controller:

ZYNQ AB, based in Stockholm, is responsible for your data.
