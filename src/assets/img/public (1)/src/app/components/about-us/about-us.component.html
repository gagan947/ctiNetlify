Who We Are

ZYNQ is a Swedish healthtech company revolutionizing how people understand and care for their skin. By combining AI-powered diagnostics with access to Sweden’s leading clinics, we make expert knowledge accessible to everyone – directly through their phone, free of charge.

Our Mission

To democratize skin diagnostics by providing everyone with instant access to professional insights – no waiting times, no bookings, no fees. We believe that scientific skincare should be as accessible as a selfie.

Our Team

Behind ZYNQ is a cross-functional team of doctors, AI researchers, and design experts. With over 20 years of experience in aesthetic medicine, technology, and clinic operations, we have launched several world-leading brands across the Swedish and Nordic markets. Our work is grounded in close collaborations with certified clinics, dermatologists, and industry specialists.
